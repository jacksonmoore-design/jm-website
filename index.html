WELCOME HOME

Hiya! Thank you for visiting my website.

I am a Chicago based artist and designer that loves patterns, textures, and deep rich colors. I work in oil, fiber, installation, and now web design. I am interested in how I can create a more tactile and sensory experience through web design while still keeping the site easy to use.

I created this website as a place for me to understand all the capabilities of web design. It features many of the things I love: travel, art, food, and gardening. As I learn more this site will serve as a playground and grow with time.

I hope you enjoy exploring the site as much as I have had fun creating it. Please let me know what you think on the Contact page.
